From Oracle to "Thinking Partner"

Old Approach (Risky)

Providing direct, definitive answers to emotionally sensitive questions like "Should I leave my partner?", risking overdependence on a machine.

New Approach (Responsible)

Offering non-directive, open-ended questions and encouraging users to consult trusted humans, redefining its role to help users navigate uncertainty.

This shift was informed by over 90 experts and reflects a new focus on responsible AI design, acknowledging that the most helpful action is sometimes not to answer directly.
